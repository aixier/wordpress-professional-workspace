There's this absolutely wild trend taking over TikTok right now - "Lolitics"! Basically, Gen Z is taking 90s British political footage and remixing it with Charli XCX or Justin Bieber tracks, turning serious debates into stan culture content.

Picture this: Tony Blair and Gordon Brown's political rivalry set to "365", complete with pink filters and heart emojis... It's absolutely unhinged! Parliamentary debates have literally become fanfiction-level psychodramas.

What's fascinating is that this isn't just mindless trolling. It's Gen Z's way of engaging with political history. Through irony, emotion, and aesthetic remixing, they're making boring political content accessible and entertaining.

The genius part? It creates a "safe" space to explore serious topics. You can scroll through hilarious videos while actually learning about historical political events. It's educational content disguised as entertainment - the perfect trojan horse!
